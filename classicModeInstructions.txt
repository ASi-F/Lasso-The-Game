This mode has two types of coins.



The gold coins increase the points by 1.



The bombs decrease the number of points by 1.
The game ends if the player catches 5 or more bomb. The user can end the game themselves by clicking on the exit button.
The number of Bombs out of 5 caught by the player currently is diplayed on the upper left side of the screen. The game
gradually becomes more difficult as the number of bombs increase.After the game ends the user is redirected to the score
board where they can see their current score, their personal high score ,the overall high score and the leaderboard.

The controls are as follows:
Press the up key to throw the lasso.
Press the down key to yank the lasso.
Press the right key to rotate the lasso anticlockwise.
Press the left key to rotate the lasso clockwise.
Press spacebar to loop the lasso.
Click on the exit button on the top right to leave the game.This will redirect the player to the score board.

Throwing action - throws the lasso loop in the direction in which the lasso is oriented.
Yank action - Pulls back the lasso loop.
Loop action - Loop the lasso to catch the nearby coins! Doesnot work when the lasso is in yanked position.
Rotating action - Rotate the lasso to change the direction of initial throw. Works only when the lasso is in yanked position
